Rentals

For beginning or less aggressive skiers who want to have fun, we carry the Rossignol Flash Series. These skis are very predictable and track nicely in a variety of terrain.

We offer an intermediate performance skis for progressive skiers who are perfecting their technique and want to ski the whole mountain in all conditions. Our performance skis this year are Rossignol Experience 83 for men, and Rossignol Temptation 76's for women.

For junior skiers this year we've greatly expanded our depth. For beginners, try Rossignol's Edge Jr skis. We have sizes from 67 cm to 140cm. For better skiers, try new Rossignol Sprayer Pro or Rossignol Scratch Pro twin tips. Sizes range from 115cm to 158cm.
