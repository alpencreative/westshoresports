West Shore Sports carries several major brands of great accessories to enhance your West Shore experience.

For all sports we have West Shore Sports logowear tees, hats & hoodies, ShreadHead trucker hats, M Shades and ONE sunglasses, and AloeGator sunscreen and lip balms.

For cycling we have WTB mountain bike tires, Gyro adult, teen and kid's helmets and gloves, and from Giant Bicycle pumps, seats, locks, tires, tubes, bells, grips, pedals, and repair parts. We also carry bike lubes from White Lightning & Tri-Flow and Giant CO2 cartridges.

For water sports we have Ocean Kayak accessories and Bic SUP paddles, ExtraSport PFD's, and Seattle Sports Dry Bags. We also sell maps, bottled water and other accessories.
