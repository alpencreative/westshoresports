Full Moon Snowshoe Strolls at Sugar Pine Point State Park

Every Saturday closest to the full moon, West Shore Sports hosts a a benefit for Sierra State Parks Foundation. These Full-Moon Snowshoe Strolls are a great way to experience the park afterhours- that is, after dusk, when the park is normally closed.

The dates for this year's Full-Moon Snowshoe Stroll are as follows:

  • January 3rd
  • January 31st
  • February 28th

For reservations, call (530)525-9920. Meet in SPPSP at 6:30pm, Strolls start at 7:00pm sharp.
