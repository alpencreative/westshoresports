West Shore Sports carries Demo Snowboards from Burton. We've got a good selection of powder, all-mountain/freeride, and camber boards.

This year, men can choose from:

  • Burton Sherlock- a Flying-V press with directional shape that is a mixture of traditional and reverse camber. Rides like traditional camber on groomers, slays powder like full-rocker. This perfect one-board quiver can shred any part of the mountain, pow or pack.
  • Burton SuperHero- a full rocker press that excels in all-mountain conditions. This twin-shaped deck is extremely playful, whether it be in the park or in the deep stuff.
  • Burton Process- a traditional camber press with twin-shape that is a blast to ride. This snappy, responsive deck excels on harpack and groomers. Perfect for dialing-in that 40-footer!

    • For women:

  • The all-mountain Deja Vu- A Flying-V press with twin shape makes this a sweet ride for those deep powder days. This board is about as versatile as it can get!

    • Stop by Mondays thru Thursdays and TRY OUT 2 DEMOS FOR THE PRICE OF ONE (2 for 1 to same person only). Our expert staff can spend extra time with you selecting a board that best suits your style and ability.

    All demos are hand tuned and hot waxed every time out!

    Kids Snowboards from Burton and K2. Beginning-to-intermediate snowboarders have a choice:

  • Burton Learn-to-Ride (LTR) snowboard with Low-Stack strap-in-bindings for a great feel. These camber snowboards are made with a beveled base to make learning easier. A beveled base helps to transition between heel and toe edges; in other words, more difficult to catch your edges, easier to learn and progress.
  • Full-rockered Cruzers, great-all-mountain boards with Progression Custom strap-in bindings for a higher-performance setup. These boards work well in a variety of conditions, from hardpack to powder.
